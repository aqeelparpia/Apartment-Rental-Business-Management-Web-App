About Us

This company first started in 2019 at Seneca College by 3 groups members: Mark Krutik, Aqeel Parpia and Daniel Ameresekere. This website is built for the building management company and it enables building mangers to file work orders for services they may need.These services may include elevator repair, window cleaning, plumbing electrical fixes and many other services. Our group decided to create a website like this one because the current business for filing work orders is on paper. Our solution will reduce the killing of trees, save money on paper, and be able to make any changes to the work order on the spot. Our web application will have a chat application for doing just that. In addition, managers will be able update, delete, view and add new workers, and to view workers accounts. Workers can view work orders and their account details as well. Security will be a big component in this web app architecture as there is a register and login functionality. This functionality will allow to users to register, which will register a user, salt and hash a users password. That way it prevents others from hacking into your account. This entire application is writting in the MEAN stack, but instead of MongoDB we will be using PostgreSQL.

Window cleaning services are services for window repair or cleaning.

Computer Technicians provide electronic keys for improved security and easier access for residents to get into their apartment.

Carpenters provide any repairs to a unit including, kitchen cabinets, walls and flooring.

Our Elevator repair services are for fixing elevators or getting people out of stuck elevators.

We also offer many more services such as; garbage collecting, fire alarm testing, building management services, cleaning services, etc. For more information on the services that we provide, please visit our Contact page. The Contact page will provide you with our address, phone number, a contact form and other means of reaching us.
